Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you register for SourcePoint, we collect the following information:

  • Full name
  • Username
  • Email address
  • Mobile number
  • Organization/Institution (optional)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and maintain our event aggregation service
  • Send SMS notifications about community events (with your consent)
  • Allow event registration and participation tracking
  • Communicate with you about events you've registered for
  • Improve and personalize your experience

3. SMS Notifications

By creating an account, you consent to receive SMS notifications about community events, outreach programs, and emergency alerts from SourcePoint and its partner organizations. You may opt out at any time by contacting us through our contact form.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your registration information with event organizers for events you have registered to attend.

5. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your personal information. Passwords are hashed and stored securely.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Update or correct your information
  • Delete your account
  • Opt out of SMS notifications

7. Contact

For any privacy-related concerns, please use our contact form.
